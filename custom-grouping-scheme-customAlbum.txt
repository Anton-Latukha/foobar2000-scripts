This groups audio tracks into consistent albums and shows info in convinient way.

The only way to show info in columns I found, is to hardcode the left alignment in chars, so the playlist grouping consistently appears when playlist uses the monospace font.

The additional spaces between columns are populated with spaces, and then spaces cut-off where next column should be inserted.

$puts(stream_kHz,$if($and($not($strcmp(%samplerate%,'44100')),$not($strcmp(%samplerate% %codec%,'48000 Opus'))),$left(%samplerate%,$sub($len(%samplerate%),3)))) $puts(stream_ch,$ifequal($info(channels),2,,$info(channels))) $puts(stream_bit,$if($not($or($strcmp($info(bitspersample),),$strcmp($info(bitspersample),'16'))),'×'$info(bitspersample))) $puts(stream_specs,[$get(stream_kHz)][$get(stream_bit)]['×'$get(stream_ch)]) $puts(originally_released,$if3(%original release date%,%originaldate%,%originalyear%,%date%)) [$cut([$cut([$cut([$cut([$cut([[%album artist% - ]['['$get(originally_released)']' ][%album%]'                                                                                                                                                                     '],80)][%RELEASETYPE%]'                                                 ',90)]['| '$if($strcmp($get(originally_released),%date%),'original',%date%)]'                       ',102)]['| '[$get(stream_specs) ][%codec%]]'                      ',120)]['| '%ALBUM DYNAMIC RANGE%]'                      ',125)]['|'[ %replaygain_album_gain%][, %replaygain_album_peak%]]
